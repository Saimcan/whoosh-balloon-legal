Whoosh Balloon — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 14 April 2026 · Last updated: 14 April 2026

1. Who we are

Whoosh Balloon ("the App") is a free-to-play casual game developed by Saimcan Sesigur ("we", "us", "the Developer"). This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights over that data. By using the App you agree to this policy.

2. Data we collect directly

We store the following data on a Firebase Realtime Database server (operated by Google Ireland Limited) to power the in-app leaderboard:

DataPurposeLinked to you?
Player ID (auto-generated hexadecimal token, not tied to Apple ID, email, or any personal identifier)Uniquely identifying your score entry so you can update your own name and scorePseudonymously — we cannot identify you by this ID alone
Display name (voluntary — chosen by you in the App)Shown next to your score on public leaderboardsOnly to the extent you choose a recognisable name
Score (integer)Ranking on daily and all-time leaderboardsVia the Player ID above

Scores are retained indefinitely for all-time ranking. Daily scores are retained for 30 days and then deleted automatically. You can request deletion at any time (see Section 6).

3. Data collected locally on your device

The following data is stored only in the App's local storage on your device and is never transmitted to us:

  • High score and last 10 runs
  • Interstitial ad cadence counters (death count, last ad timestamp)
  • Remove Ads purchase flag (if you bought the IAP)
  • ATT prompt status (whether you have been shown the tracking prompt)

This data is deleted when you uninstall the App or clear its local storage.

4. Data collected by third-party partners

4.1 Google AdMob (advertising)

The App uses Google AdMob to display interstitial ads and support a free-to-play business model. If you grant tracking permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt, AdMob may collect the following to serve personalised advertising:

  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, AAID on Android)
  • Device information (model, OS version, language, country, network type)
  • Advertising data (which ads were shown, clicked, or dismissed)

If you deny tracking permission, AdMob will serve non-personalised ads and will not collect the identifiers above for tracking purposes.

AdMob's own privacy policy governs its use of this data:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.2 Apple App Store (in-app purchases)

If you purchase the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase, the transaction is processed entirely by Apple. We never see or store your payment details. We only receive an anonymous entitlement confirmation used to disable ads on your device.

5. What we do NOT collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, physical address, precise location, contacts, photos, health data, financial data, browsing history outside the App, or any other personally identifying information.

6. Your rights

6.1 GDPR (European Economic Area, United Kingdom, Switzerland)

If you are in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Erase your data (the "right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time — you can revoke AdMob tracking in iOS at any time via Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Whoosh Balloon
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority

To exercise these rights, contact us at the address in Section 10.

6.2 CCPA (California residents)

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, to request deletion, and to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. However, sharing device identifiers with AdMob for personalised advertising may be considered "sharing" under the CPRA. You can opt out by denying the ATT prompt or by toggling tracking off in iOS Settings.

7. Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Security

Leaderboard data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored on Firebase servers with access controlled by Firebase Security Rules. No system is 100% secure, but we follow industry-standard practices to protect your data.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be announced via an in-App notification.
