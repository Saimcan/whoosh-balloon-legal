What data do we store?

Whoosh Balloon stores the following data on our servers (Firebase Realtime Database):

DataRetention
Player ID (auto-generated anonymous token)Until deletion requested
Display name (chosen by you)Until deletion requested
Score (integer)All-time: until deletion requested · Daily: 30 days

We do not store your name, email, phone number, location, or any other personally identifying information.

Data stored on your device only

The following data is stored locally on your device and is never sent to us:

  • High score and last 10 runs
  • Ad cadence counters
  • Remove Ads purchase flag
  • Ad tracking consent status

You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's storage in your device settings.

How to request deletion of server data

  1. Send an email to saimcan.sesigur@gmail.com
  2. Include the subject line: Data Deletion Request — Whoosh Balloon
  3. If you know your in-game display name, include it in the email to help us locate your data
  4. We will delete your Player ID, display name, and all associated scores from our database
  5. You will receive a confirmation email within 48 hours
